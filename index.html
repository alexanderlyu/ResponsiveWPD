Marvel-verse Iron Man (2019) featuring several past Iron Man stories


Iron Man is a superhero created for Marvel Comics who has appeared in various comics, movies, video games, and animations. The character rose to worldwide popularity in the film Iron Man (2008) and its subsequent films, portrayed by Robert Downey Jr. As a technology based superhero, his powers coming from the various suits that he creates. Iron Man is characterized by his genius intellect, perseverance and egotism.
